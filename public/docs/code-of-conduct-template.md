# PMA Code of Conduct Template (Sample)

This template is provided for educational use only. It is not legal advice.

## Purpose

This Code of Conduct establishes expected behavior, member safety standards, and response procedures for violations.

## Member Expectations

- Treat all members and guests with respect.
- Avoid threatening, abusive, or illegal behavior.
- Protect private member information and private spaces.
- Follow vendor and event safety rules.

## Progressive Enforcement

1. First violation: documented warning.
2. Second violation: warning plus temporary suspension.
3. Third violation: formal review and vote or executive decision according to bylaws.

## Severe Violations

Immediate suspension or removal may apply for violence, theft, intentional property damage, or other severe misconduct.

## Guest Policy

- Guests must follow the same behavioral standards.
- Members are responsible for guests they invite.

## Incident Documentation

Maintain records of incidents, responses, and outcomes for internal governance.

## Appeals

Describe how members may appeal decisions and the timeline for review.
