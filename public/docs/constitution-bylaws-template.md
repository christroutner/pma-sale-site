# PMA Constitution and Bylaws Template (Sample)

This template is provided for educational use only. It is not legal advice.

## Article I: Name

The name of this organization is: ______________________

## Article II: Mission

Describe your association mission and core purpose.

## Article III: Membership

- Eligibility requirements
- Admission process
- Member categories
- Resignation and revocation process

## Article IV: Dues and Financial Rules

- Initial and recurring dues
- Late fee policy
- Voting and approval process for fee changes

## Article V: Governance

- Officers and duties
- Elections and appointment rules
- Board or committee structure

## Article VI: Meetings and Voting

- Meeting cadence
- Quorum definition
- Voting thresholds

## Article VII: Member Conduct

Reference a separate Code of Conduct and enforcement process.

## Article VIII: Amendments

Define how this constitution may be updated.

## Article IX: Dissolution

Define process for closing the association and handling remaining assets.
